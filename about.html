About

After The Great Purchasing by T. Martin Industries, The Muskrats (Xavier, Josh, Konnor, and Alan) find themselves split apart, subject to monotonous office work for the rest of their careers.

10 years pass, and as if the stars aligned, Alan and Josh find themselves working together again after another company change, with some new faces to join the fray.

As per company tradition, the new gang is thrown onto a safari trip in Africa: a team-building adventure in the waiting.

Unbeknownst to them, there's a bit more to the trip than what meets the eye...

A story of bonds, betrayal, and mystery... A safari gone wrong.
